Our Clients

See What our Clients Have to say About BUNKA

Manufacturing Supervisor

Over the last 24 years with different med device companies, I have been through numerous leadership courses over those years and most were less than a week and back to your job, with no follow up. Over the last weeks with BUNKA, we have had time to learn, grow, execute and more importantly follow up with Monika, Steve and our coaches.
The small classes offered a chance to feel comfortable and ask the questions that you would not have asked with 20 people in the class. Monika was able to focus on each of us and was able to stop if she saw a blank look on one of our faces. It was nice to learn that even though we were all from different departments we shared many of the same thoughts, concerns or obstacles with our jobs on a daily basis. It’s has been nice getting to know everyone and I look forward to working with them after BUNKA

Principal Reliability Engineer

Mastermind. That is the word that best describes, in my mind, what we have become throughout this journey. We have grown as leaders, coworkers and friends. We have learned to listen, understand, question, reflect, build and maintain relationships but most importantly we learned that we are not a finished product and there is always room for improvement.
BUNKA will help bring down silos. Focus work. Find and mitigate risks. Driving action in the right direction. BUNKA is an operating system, it is a platform that provides the tools to supports and promote people growth and development.

Senior Quality Systems Manager

I felt the power of the Bunka coaching techniques during the Regulatory Audit. Monika and Steve were available for us to pop in and unload our concerns doubts etc., as well as give us support and direction. They did not tell us what we should do, but their coaching gave us the permission and confidence to do what we already knew we had to do to succeed.
Being BUNKA means you have been enlightened to the power of consciously thinking about the space between the metrics. When you go into BUNKA mode, you are thinking about “how and why” we are where we are and applying methodical tools and practices to determine the best path forward to achieve things you otherwise would have thought were impossible.

Plant Director

After going through the BUNKA training I realize that BUNKA is actually a Verb.  It is how we should be conducting ourselves on a daily basis.  It’s a mindset – the way we should be – it’s in our actions
