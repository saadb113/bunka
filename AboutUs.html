Meet our Partners

Monika Sumra, Phd

Managing Partner

Dr. Monika Sumra is a business anthropologist and entrepreneur, decorated academic, and organizational culture expert. She has focused her career helping organizations excel in their leadership and operational efforts saving her clients over $1 Billion. Monika is co-founder of BUNKA™, a business improvement organization focused on leadership growth and cultural alignment to deliver results. She is also co-developer of the BUNKA™ KATA Method for Problem-Solving and Continuous Improvement., She is highly regarded for her expertise improving organizational quality mindset/culture in manufacturing environments.


With over 20 years of leadership and team-building experience, Monika is a highly sought-after performance leadership coach who specializes in driving results through assessing and improving individual performance and organizational culture.


In November 2019 Monika completed her doctoral studies at the University of Toronto specializing in the area of leadership and leadership behaviors. She also holds an MSc in women studies and anthropology, and a BSc in women’s health and sociocultural anthropology. Monika is also a Lean Six Sigma Black Belt, published author, researcher, teacher, and scientist.

Steve Sumra, MBA

Senior Partner

Steve Sumra is a successful strategic performance advisor and management consultant since 1999 primarily in the areas of behaviors and sustainable performance and has helped his clients save over $3 Billion. He is highly sought after for his expertise in improving quality in manufacturing and industrial maintenance.


He is a highly experienced Lean and world-class Toyota Kata Master Coach as well co-founder of BUNKA™ and co-developer of the BUNKA™ KATA Method for Problem-Solving and Continuous Improvement. Steve has led hundreds of successful BUNKA™ KATAs in diverse industries globally. From food manufacturing, defense equipment, oil and gas, telecom to pharmaceutical and medical devices, Steve has been able to adapt the BUNKA™ KATA approach in many contexts and organizational environments.


Developing strategies focused on everyday practice towards creating “muscle memory” to embed lean practice within organizational culture is one of Steve’s many valued strengths. He holds an MBA from the University of Nottingham, UK as well as a BSc in manufacturing.

Harry Fouke, MBA

Senior Partner

Harry joined BUNKA in July of 2022 reuniting him with the firm’s founders having worked with them in various capacities since 1999. Harry brings extensive experience serving clients in both FDA and USDA regulated industries leading cost and quality transformations globally.


Prior to joining BUNKA, he served as the COO of deverus, an HR Technology SaaS provider and still serves in an advisory role. Before joining deverus, Harry worked in the management consulting industry as President of the Americas for Alexander Proudfoot, leading the firm’s operations in North and South America with an emphasis on people, process, and systems. Previously at Proudfoot, as well as in senior positions with Hitachi (Celerant) and The Highland Group, he supported clients on numerous corporate operational strategy projects, including: private equity due diligences and post merger integrations, growth strategy design and implementation, cost savings programs, and industrial operational excellence programs globally.


Harry holds an MBA from The Cox School of Business at Southern Methodist University, as well as a B.S. from Texas A&M University in addition to completing the High Impact Leadership program at Columbia University.

Raul Diaz, ScD

Senior Partner

Raul, the former Global Quality Lead at Merck's Animal Health North America, retired from the position in December 2022 after serving since 2020. Before this role, he held the position of Operations Director at Merck, Sharp & Dohme’s (MSD) Animal Health Bio-pharmaceutical operations in Montes Claros, Brazil. Earlier in his career, Raul led MSD's Xochimilco, Human Health Pharmaceutical Operations, near Mexico City, and served as Plant Manager at Merck’s Bio-pharmaceutical campus in Elkton, VA.


Throughout his 30-year career at Merck, Raul gained expertise in both Human and Animal Health, working with small and large molecules, including sterile/non-sterile pharmaceuticals, bacterial and viral vaccines, and biological contamination control. He is well-versed in lean/six sigma practices, change methodologies, strategic planning, development, and execution.


Before his role in Elkton, VA, Raul served as the Global Leader of Strategy Development and Realization for the Merck Manufacturing Division (MMD). In this capacity, he played a key role in developing strategic plans to advance the manufacturing organization's vision, mission, and objectives, fostering revenue profitability and growth.

Raul holds degrees in biochemistry from the University of Puerto Rico, the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Rutgers University, and the University of South Carolina’s School of Pharmacy.
